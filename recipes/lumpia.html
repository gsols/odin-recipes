Lumpia Recipe

A popular Filipino dish consisting of a thin crepe filled with a mixture of meat and vegetables, then rolled and fried until crispy. Lumpia is often served with a sweet and sour dipping sauce.

This recipe can be easily made at home.

This is a beginner friendly recipe. So, hop in!

Ingredients

  • Lumpia wrappers
  • Ground pork
  • Potato, grated
  • Carrots, grated
  • Onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine ground pork, grated potato, grated carrots, chopped onion, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Take a lumpia wrapper and place a spoonful of the filling near one edge. Fold the sides over the filling and roll tightly to form a cylinder. Seal the edge with a bit of water.
  3. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Fry the lumpia until golden brown and crispy on all sides.
  4. Remove from oil and drain on paper towels. Serve hot with sweet and sour sauce or vinegar dipping sauce.
