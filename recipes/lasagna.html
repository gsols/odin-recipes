Lasagna Recipe

A classic Italian baked pasta dish made from wide, flat sheets of pasta layered with sauces (like meat ragù or béchamel), cheeses (ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan), and seasonings, then baked until bubbly and golden, resulting in a hearty, comforting casserole with rich, savory, and creamy flavors.

This recipe can be easily made at home.

This is a beginner friendly recipe. So, hop in!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Eggs
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and minced garlic, sautéing until translucent.
  3. Add ground beef or sausage to the skillet, cooking until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, eggs, and a pinch of salt.
  6. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Place lasagna noodles over the sauce, then spread a layer of the ricotta mixture, followed by mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  9. Let it cool for 10 minutes before slicing. Serve and enjoy!
