Omelette Recipe

A classic dish made by whisking eggs and cooking them in a frying pan, often folded around a filling such as cheese, vegetables, or meats.

This recipe can be easily made at home.

This is a beginner friendly recipe. So, hop in!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Bell peppers, chopped
  • Onion, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter or oil for cooking
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk the eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the egg mixture and cook until the edges start to set.
  4. Add cheese, bell peppers, and onion on one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold the other half over the filling and cook for another minute.
  6. Slide onto a plate and serve hot.
